iOS Notification Guide
1
Add to Home Screen first — Notifications only work when installed as a PWA. Open in Safari → Share → "Add to Home Screen".
2
Requires iOS 16.4+ — Check Settings → General → About for your iOS version.
3
If blocked — Go to iOS Settings → StudyBoard → Notifications and toggle on.
4
App must be open — Web notifications fire while the app is open or recently backgrounded.
